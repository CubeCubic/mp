# Cube Cubic

Hey, welcome to Cube Cubic! It's a spot online that's all about music, awesome sounds, and good vibes. It's more than just a player; it's like a bunch of feelings put into music.

So, why Cube Cubic?

Tons of Choices: There are lots of different songs in all sorts of styles – jazz, funk, rock, you name it. You can find something totally new or listen to old favorites.

Music You Understand: There's a focus on songs and poems from Georgia that are really moving. You can really feel the words and music together.

Easy to look at interface: The site looks cool. It's got a Deep Ocean theme that makes it chill to listen to tunes. Finding albums and stuff is super easy.

Cool visuals: Each song has artwork that goes with it, so you get a better feel for the music.

Lyrics are Easy: You can read the words to the songs right there, so you really get what they're about.

Simple and always available: The music player is always there, and you can change the volume and skip around easily. It works great on phones and computers.

Share with Friends: If you find something you like, you can send it to your buddies quick!

Open and Clear: The whole thing is out in the open on GitHub Pages, so anyone can check it out and even help make it better.

Cube Cubic is where music becomes something that inspires you. Come join us, listen to new stuff, and just get lost in the music. Hit Play and enjoy!
